## Conference Talks

- **Multilevel Monte Carlo Method with Meta-Model for Advection–Diffusion Problems**  
  *MCQMC 2022 – Monte Carlo and Quasi-Monte Carlo Methods in Scientific Computing*, Linz, Austria — *July 2022*  
  Presented research on modeling advection–diffusion processes using a combination of the Multilevel Monte Carlo (MLMC) method and deep learning surrogates to accelerate uncertainty quantification.  
  [Conference website](https://www.ricam.oeaw.ac.at/events/conferences/mcqmc2022/)

- **Groundwater Flow Meta-Model for Multilevel Monte Carlo Methods**  
  *ITAT 2021 – 21st Conference on Information Technologies – Applications and Theory*, Hotel Heľpa, Nízke Tatry & Muránska Planina, Slovakia — *September 2021*  
  Presented a surrogate approach to reduce the computational cost of Monte Carlo simulations in groundwater flow modeling.  
  [Conference website](https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-2962/)

## Poster Presentations

- **Deep Learning Surrogate for Efficient Upscaling in 3D Fractured Media**  
  *ML Prague 2025*, Prague, Czech Republic — *April 2025*  
  Presented research on convolutional neural network–based surrogates for numerical homogenization of hydraulic conductivity tensors in 3D fractured porous media.  
  [Conference website](https://www.mlprague.com/prague2025)

- **Generative Models for Bone Dataset Augmentation**  
  *GeMSS 2024 – Generative Modeling Summer School*, Eindhoven, The Netherlands — *June 2024*  
  Presented research on denoising diffusion models for graphs to generate realistic 3D bone structures, aimed at improving data diversity and augmentation in medical imaging applications.  
  [Event website](https://gemss.ai/2024/)
